About

Hanuman Timbers

Hanuman Timbers started its journey a few years back, in 1982 as a timber trading organization. Founded by Mr. Ramesh Patel, eventually being run by his sons. The firm is purely a family run business. The high level of ethics and customer service put the firm to its fast paced growth over the short span of its existence. The high level of regards and respect to its associates and Employees have been its highest strong hold and achievement. Skill, high sense of turn-around time,Courteousness and Good Customer service offered by one and all at Hanuman Timbers, make us the dealer of choice for all our customers. In a nut shell, experience at Hanuman Timbers would be a refreshingly different one for any who chooses to do business with us.
